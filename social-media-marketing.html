Organic traffic is website visitors that were referred by organic, or unpaid, results of search engines, principally Google. Paid traffic is website visitors that were referred by paid campaigns such as Google Ads or Facebook Ads
Pay per click is a type of advertising where an organisation pays each time a user clicks on an advertisement. The costs incurred during a PPC campaign vary based on the competitiveness of the target keyword.
Both have their benefits depending on your marketing objective and budget. We often find that businesses would rather build communities over high budget PPC campaigns. If you are a smaller outfit with limited budgets we would recommend an 'organic' approach rather than 'paid'.
