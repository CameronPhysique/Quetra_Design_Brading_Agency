From cleaning services to barbers, restaurants to personal trainers, we help businesses like yours stand out in a crowd. Whether it's a new website or boujee business card, let's create something special.

Great visual designers understand that creating well-designed websites is much more than just aesthetics. Besides slick visuals, great design is actually about creating a website that is also functional and thus creates a good user experience.


There’s a process or approach that needs to be followed to make sure both form and function are taken into account. There are so many web design processes out there, but we're going to specifically share our internal design process and how we custom build websites from the ground up to suite your requirements.

Define

At this discovery phase, We usually work closely with clients. The goals of our initial steps are to get as much information as possible and to understand the bigger picture of your project. Typically a basic plan is worked through highlighting areas for improvement, we will also go away and research the competitors to get general ideas of what designs respond well to your audience, and how to set you apart from the rest.

Design

Depending on the complexity of the project, we either begin by sketching out design ideas to get some rough insight into the general layout and functionality. A larger and more complex project requires wireframes, so there’s a translatable visual representation of the site map. The planning is detailed and thorough in preparation for the next step, which is visual design.

Build

This is the fun part! We begin by bringing in colors, images, and typography adding 'meat to the bones'. During the design exploration, We typically come up with two or three alternative options and start honing in on the design based on the feedback from the client to manoeuvre towards the final design. We also ask for alternative client participants to choose some design options they prefer, so they feel that they are also invested in the success of the design and we have broader insight.

When there are complex inter-activities involved in the design, we build a prototype to show how certain elements work in the design and how the navigation works so it can be better understood. The more communication we can get along the process, the better the project result.

Launch

Usually, it takes two or three rounds of revision before the design is approved. Once the design is finalised, it is time to hand off the design mocks and assets to developers so they can complete building out the back end of your site. Your website then undergoes a testing phase where we go through each element closely navigating through the links to ensure all functionallity is operating as expected.

A few days before the launch, designers will be working alongside developers to make sure the design is built according to the given specs. Once the site is launched, it’s time to perform Quality Assurance (QA) on the site and backlog any bugs or missing items. We then provide free maintainence on all our websites for (3 months) complimentary.

What we do

Our services are designed to help your business stand out and turn your ideas into digital realities.

Brand Identity

A new brand identity represents a major investment for your company; it may also signify a change of direction or other important strategic change. To ensure that key stakeholders in your business – employees, customers, suppliers, distributors, owners and the media – understand and support the change, you must develop a comprehensive launch plan. Announcing a new brand makes your audience aware; it must also ensure that staff who indoctrinate the identity at work apply it consistently and align with brand values.

Web Design

A web designer works on the appearance, layout, and, in some cases, content of a website. Appearance, for instance, relates to the colors, font, and images used. Layout refers to how information is structured and categorized. A good web design is easy to use, aesthetically pleasing, and suits the user group and brand of the website. Many webpages are designed with a focus on simplicity, so that no extraneous information and functionality that might distract or confuse users appears. Removing as many potential points of user frustration as possible is a critical consideration.

Marketing

Digital marketing is the marketing and advertising of a business, person, product, or service using online channels, electronic devices, and digital technologies. A few examples of digital marketing include social media, email, pay-per-click (PPC), search engine optimisation (SEO), Affiliate Marketing, Social Media Marketing (SMM), Influencer Marketing and more.

Digital marketing is important because it connects a business with its customers or clients when they're online, & is effective in all industries. It connects businesses with ideal customers when they are on Google through SEO & PPC, on social media with social media marketing, & through email with email marketing.

Video Editing

Do your YouTube videos need intros? Will a YouTube intro boost the professionalism of your videos and help them stand out on the crowded platform? Absolutely. Give your videos the best chance to get noticed with compelling video intros, made by Quetra. Re-enforce cohesive branding with a professional and consistent look cross platform.

If your intro is fun and recognisable, people are more likely to engage with and share your content. Be open to experimentation — study the performance of your videos, the comments, and key metrics like where people stop watching. Adjust accordingly.

UI/UX Design

Our UX designers are trained to think like a user, providing a clear pathway to help the user navigate your website and how they interact with content. Our developers aim is to place users in control of the interface or website, make it fun & comfortable to interact with a product or service. Make navigation simple to reduce cognitive load and ensuring designs are interfaces are consistent across platforms and devices.

UX design is all about identifying and solving user problems by creating intuitive, aesthetically-pleasing, interactive interfaces.

Management

Website management is the collection of processes used to ensure your website is professional, up-to-date, and functioning as intended. It encompasses general maintenance, security, and long-term development of the website, as well as organising your content delivery and marketing strategy.

Social media management is the process of managing your online presence on social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok and YouTube by creating, publishing, and analysing content you post. Managing social media also includes engaging and interacting with social media users.
